Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American dish that consists of breaded chicken breast covered in marinara sauce and mozzarella, parmesan, or provolone cheese. It is then baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place flour in a shallow dish. Beat eggs with water in another shallow dish. Combine bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese in a third shallow dish.
  3. Coat each chicken breast in flour, then dip in egg mixture, and finally coat in bread crumb mixture.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Transfer chicken to a baking dish. Top each chicken breast with marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil.
  6. Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 20 minutes.
